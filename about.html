About The Rub

The idea

Everybody loves a massage.

The problem is, it can be expensive and inconvenient to book appointments. Sometimes you just want a decent backrub to release some tension from your muscles or calm frayed nerves.

At The Rub you can simply pop in to our lovely shop and have an effective 10, 20 or 30 minute backrub with no appointment required. We aim to provide a service which is Affordable, Convenient, and Effective.

Pop in and try a backrub some time soon!

The routine we use is second to none. It was designed by the founder of one of the major Shiatsu colleges in Europe.

The routine uses a very effective series of movements, designed to release tension in the upper back, neck and shoulders — where we all need it most!

All of our practitioners are fully professionally qualified and insured. Most people will really benefit from a regular backrub — pop in and find out!

Our shop at 15 Pottergate is open Monday to Saturday, 10am–6pm. We are also trialling opening on Sundays from 11am–4pm — come down for a relaxing Sunday backrub and start the week refreshed and energised!

The backrub service is suitable for a very wide range of people and age groups. We have happily given backrubs to young children and people advanced in years.

We modify the way we work to suit the age and physical condition of each client. Most people will find the backrub effective and enjoyable.

Just pop in or contact us to learn more.
